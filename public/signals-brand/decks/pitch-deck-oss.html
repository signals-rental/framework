SIG-F10Self-HostingRev A

Self-Host Without Compromise

Your data, your servers, your infrastructure. Self-hosting is a first-class experience, not a second-class workaround.

Bare Metal / VPS
Any Linux server with PHP 8.4+, PostgreSQL, and Nginx or Caddy. Traditional deployment with full control. From £5/month.

Section 04 — Co-Branding
BRD-005d Co-Branding REV A

Co-Branding Rules

How the Signals mark pairs with a partner brand. Equal visual weight, consistent spacing, Signals always on the left.

Co-Branding Lockup
Signals
Partner MIN. CLEARSPACE: 2× EQUAL VISUAL WEIGHT
  • Signals mark always appears on the left side of the lockup
  • Equal visual weight between Signals and partner marks
  • Minimum 2× clear space between marks (relative to mark height)
  • Vertical divider line separates the two marks at 1px, using --grey-border
  • Partner marks must not exceed the height of the Signals mark
  • Corner accent color on Signals mark remains the product-appropriate colour
